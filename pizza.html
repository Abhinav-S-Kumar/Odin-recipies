Pizza

Description

Pizza is a crispy and cheesy Italian dish made with a dough base, tomato sauce, toppings, and lots of melted cheese. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olives
  • Capsicum
  • Oregano & chili flakes

Steps

  1. Roll the dough and spread tomato sauce over it.
  2. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  3. Bake until cheese melts and crust becomes golden.
  4. Slice and serve hot.
