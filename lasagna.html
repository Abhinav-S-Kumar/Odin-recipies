Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian layered pasta dish filled with sauce, meat, and cheese. Itâ€™s rich, comforting, and perfect for family gatherings or weekend meals.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Garlic, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Cook ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until the top turns golden and bubbly.
