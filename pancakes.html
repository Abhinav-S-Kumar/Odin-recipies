Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats made from a simple batter and served with butter, fruit, chocolate, or maple syrup. Quick to prepare and loved by everyone!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and eggs; mix to form a smooth batter.
  3. Pour batter onto a heated pan and cook both sides golden.
  4. Serve with butter, honey, syrup, or fruits.
